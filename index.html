FAQs:

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are where your brilliant ideas are turbocharged into reality. Build anything and everything! The possibilities are limitless. Make something, break something, do something you've always wanted to but never got the chance to. We help you meet other motivated participants as crazy as you and together, you'll make your dreams a reality.

What if I don't have a team or an idea?

Doesn't matter! Many hackers don't have fully formed teams or ideas when they come. That's where we come in. We'll have team-forming events at the start of the event to help match you to teams and ideas. Then, the real fun starts!

Who can attend?

Anyone can attend! The bigger the mix, the better! We want to help bring together motivated participants from all fields whether they're students or working professionals. Then, we'll put your ideas into the pressure cooker!

How much does this cost?

This event will be low-cost. Meaning you pay $15 to attend, and upon confirmation that you've attended on the day itself, we'll refund you $10, effectively making this...$5!

How do teams work?

Teams will comprise of 4-5 hackers. We'll have a team-forming event at the start to help match the ideas to the people needed!

What can I build?

Anything. Whether it's hardware, software, assistive tech, mobile solutions, we welcome all of it (within the realms of healthcare that is)! Ideas will be judged by our panel of judges based on their creativity, utility, as well as their feasibility.

What should I bring?

Hackers neeed to bring any form of identification for registration, themselves, and any devices you think you may need during the hackathon (p.s. your laptops are highly recommended!)

What will I eat?

We'll br providing the food. Please DO let us know if you have any special dietary requirements during registration!

Where do I sleep?

Now, usual hackathons run overnight and some participants go through the night without sleep. HOWEVER, being a healthcare based hackathon, we'll strongly recommend participants take a break, go home and get some rest at the end of the day.
